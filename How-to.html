Overview

Our gaming platform includes two most played fun games: the Simple Quiz Game and Tic Tac Toe.

1.1 Simple Quiz Game

The Simple Quiz Game challenges your knowledge on various topics, specifically in Nigeria. Answer questions correctly to earn points and emerge as the quiz champion.

1.2 Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic strategy game for two players. Outwit your opponent and get three of your symbols in a row to win the game.

2. Simple Quiz Game

2.1 Playing the Quiz

  • Start the quiz by clicking the “start button”.
  • You are allowed to answer questions at your convenient time but remember! Cheating is not good.
  • Proceed with the next question until all questions are answered.

2.2 Scoring and Winner

  • Keep track of points for each correct answer.
  • Tally up the points after all questions are answered.
  • Display your score.
  • Note that the answers will not be revealed.

3. Tic Tac Toe

3.1 Rules

  • Players take turns placing their symbols in empty cells.
  • The goal is to get three of your symbols in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

3.2 Playing Tic Tac Toe

  • There are two symbols "X" and "O."
  • Decide who will play as "X" and "O."
  • The player with "X" goes first, placing their symbol in any empty cell.
  • Players alternate turns until one achieves three symbols in a row or all cells are filled.

3.3 Determining the Winner

  • If a player gets three symbols in a row, they win the game.
  • If the grid fills up without a winner, the game ends in a draw.

Conclusion

You are now ready to enjoy the Simple Quiz Game and play a thrilling round of Tic Tac Toe. Have fun, challenge your mind, and share these entertaining games with friends and family!

Remember, gaming is all about enjoyment, learning, and friendly competition. May the odds be in your favor!
